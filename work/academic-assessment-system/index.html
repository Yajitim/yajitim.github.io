Automated Academic Assessment & Performance Tracking System

Developed automated Excel-based tracking systems and standardized data validation models for national examinations (NABTEB/NACO) serving over 70+ students annually.

🚀

Project Overview

During my tenure as HOD ICT / ICT Data Lead at St. Joseph’s Science and Technical College Makurdi, I built automated assessment reporting systems that transformed how student performance data was tracked, analyzed, and delivered to stakeholders.

Impact & Key Highlights

  • Process Automation: Designed automated Excel assessment templates with data validation rules, cutting reporting time by over 50%.
  • National Exam Coordination: Led administration of formative and summative national assessments (NABTEB/NACO) with 100% testing guideline compliance.
  • Early Intervention Analytics: Analyzed performance trend data to identify at-risk students, enabling teachers to launch targeted intervention strategies.
