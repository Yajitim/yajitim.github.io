## Project Overview

As the **Project Statistician** for the World Bank Assisted **SFTAS-DLI 11.5 Project** at the Benue Geographic Information System (BENGIS), I engineered comprehensive spatial datasets for landed properties across major urban centers in Benue State (Makurdi, Gboko, and Otukpo).

This high-stakes data initiative was pivotal in facilitating Benue State's successful qualification for a **$2.5 Million World Bank grant**.

## Key Achievements & Deliverables

- **Spatial Data Architecture**: Developed and managed spatial datasets using **QGIS** and **QFIELD** for field data collection across Benue State.
- **Field Operations & Capacity Building**: Delivered comprehensive technical training and operational support to field personnel on QGIS/QFIELD data collection protocols.
- **Verification & Quality Assurance**: Conducted rigorous data cleaning and verification protocols on thousands of incoming field entries, correcting anomalies before loading to the state database.
- **Interactive KPI Monitoring Dashboard**: Built interactive dashboards tracking project Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) for weekly progress reporting to World Bank focal teams.

## Technologies Used

- **Spatial Tools**: QGIS, QFIELD, Spatial SQL
- **Reporting & Dashboards**: Automated Excel (Power Query/Pivot), Power BI
- **Methodologies**: Data Validation Rules, Field Verification Protocols, Encrypted Access Control
