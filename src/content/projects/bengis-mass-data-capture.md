## Project Overview

At the **Benue Geographic Information System (BENGIS)** Mass Data Capture Unit, I led the optimization of high-volume data ingestion pipelines, digitizing and securing over 3,000 high-value records on a weekly basis.

## Key Technical Contributions

- **Workflow Optimization**: Streamlined digitization processes to increase throughput while maintaining zero-tolerance data accuracy thresholds.
- **Privacy & Safeguarding**: Enforced strict data ethics and security protocols utilizing multi-level restricted access and encrypted storage to safeguard PII (Personally Identifiable Information).
- **IT Staff Training**: Designed technical training modules for IT personnel and incoming staff to ensure standardized data entry and database integrity across the department.
